About the Bunker & Collaborators

RazzBunker is a creative site which presents various artists portfolios and their illustrations.
Created for the purpose of sharing works and to have some "safe" in the art expression. In this site you can also found some web-comics, alpha-projects and experiments that aren't shared in the mass social media.
In this Bunker you can contact with the artist in interest to ask their open commissions, prices and collab suggestions.
This is site is developed by SonRazz and some collaborators.
If you wanna join to the Bunker or become an Associate please contact first to SonRazz in RazzBunker Discord and follow their instructions.
Any question of the Terms of Services or for any business purpouse you are free to send your doubts in: contact@razzbunker.com
